# Stadium Navigation

- Gate A: Entrance for Sections 100-120, nearest to Metro Station North
- Gate B: Entrance for Sections 200-220, nearest to main parking lot
- Gate C: Entrance for Sections 300-320, VIP and hospitality entrance
- Gate D: Accessible entrance for Sections 100-320, has ramps and elevators
- Food court is located on the concourse level, accessible from all gates
- First aid stations are located near Gate B and Gate D
